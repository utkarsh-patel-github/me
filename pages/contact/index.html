Frequently Asked Questions

Are all tools really free to use?

Yes, all of our core tools are completely free to use. We're committed to providing accessible tools for everyone. We may offer premium features or advanced versions of some tools in the future, but the basic functionality will always remain free.

How can I suggest a new tool?

We love hearing your ideas! You can suggest a new tool by using the contact form on this page. Select "Tool Suggestion" from the subject dropdown and describe the tool you'd like to see. Our team reviews all suggestions and prioritizes based on user demand.

Is my data secure when using your tools?

Absolutely. Most of our tools process data directly in your browser, which means your information never leaves your device. For tools that require server processing, we use secure, encrypted connections and never store your data longer than necessary. You can read our full privacy policy for more details.

Can I use these tools offline?

Currently, our tools require an internet connection to load initially. However, some tools may continue to function after loading, even if your internet connection drops. We're working on developing Progressive Web App (PWA) versions of our most popular tools to enable full offline functionality.

How do I report a bug or issue?

If you encounter a bug or issue with any of our tools, please use the contact form and select "Report a Bug" from the subject dropdown. Include as much detail as possible, including the specific tool, your device, browser, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are also very helpful!

Do you offer API access to your tools?

We're currently developing API access for some of our most popular tools. If you're interested in integrating our tools into your application or website, please contact us with details about your use case, and we'll be happy to discuss possibilities.
