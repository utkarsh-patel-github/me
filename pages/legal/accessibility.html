Our Commitment to Accessibility

Daily Tools is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We believe that the internet should be available and accessible to anyone, and we're committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology.

Conformance Status

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Conformance with these guidelines helps make the web more user-friendly for everyone.

The guidelines have three levels of accessibility (A, AA, and AAA). We've chosen Level AA as our target.

Accessibility Features

Daily Tools includes the following accessibility features:

Keyboard Accessibility

All functionality can be operated using a keyboard for users who can't use a mouse. We ensure proper focus states and logical tab order.

Text Alternatives

We provide text alternatives for non-text content, such as images, with appropriate alternative text (alt text).

Color and Contrast

Our website is designed with sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds to ensure that content is readable by people with low vision or color blindness.

Resizable Text

Our website allows users to resize text up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.

Consistent Navigation

Our navigation is consistent across the website to help users locate content more easily.

Dark Mode

We offer a dark mode option to reduce eye strain and improve readability for some users, particularly in low-light environments.

Descriptive Link Text

We use descriptive link text to help users understand where a link will take them without having to rely on surrounding context.

Responsive Design

Our website is responsive and adjusts to various screen sizes and orientations, making it accessible across different devices.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Daily Tools is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (like JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, and TalkBack)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard alternatives

We use semantic HTML to ensure that our content is properly structured and can be interpreted correctly by assistive technologies.

Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure that Daily Tools is accessible to all users, there may be some limitations:

  • Some of our older content may not yet fully comply with the latest accessibility standards. We are working to update this content.
  • Some interactive tools may have complex functionality that is challenging to make fully accessible. We are continuously working to improve these tools.
  • Third-party content that we do not control may not be fully accessible.

Advertisements

Our website displays advertisements through Google AdSense and other advertising platforms to support our free tools and services. Please note:

  • Advertisements are served by third parties and may not adhere to the same accessibility standards as our website content.
  • We have limited control over the format and content of these advertisements.
  • If you encounter accessibility issues with advertisements, we encourage you to report them to us, and we will forward your concerns to our advertising partners.

We strive to ensure that advertisements do not interfere with the overall accessibility of our website, and we work with advertising partners who share our commitment to web accessibility.

Assessment Methods

Daily Tools assesses the accessibility of our website through the following methods:

  • Self-evaluation by our development team
  • Automated testing using accessibility evaluation tools
  • User testing with participants who use assistive technologies
  • Periodic third-party audits by accessibility experts
