How to Use

1

Enter your date of birth

Select your birth date using the date picker in the first field.

2

Choose comparison date

By default, we use the current date. Uncheck "Use Today's Date" to calculate your age on a different date.

3

View your results

Click "Calculate Age" to see your exact age in years, months, and days, plus additional information.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

Our age calculator is extremely accurate. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you precise results to the day.

Can I calculate age between any two dates?

Yes! Uncheck "Use Today's Date" to select any comparison date you want. This allows you to calculate age at any point in time.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store, track, or share any dates you enter.
