About Cookie Policies

A cookie policy is a legal document that explains how your website uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and store information. It's required by privacy laws like:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) in the EU
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) in California
  • PECR (Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations) in the UK
  • Similar laws in other jurisdictions

Important Notes:

  • This tool generates a basic cookie policy template
  • Consider having a legal professional review the policy
  • Keep your cookie policy updated as your practices change
  • Make sure the policy is easily accessible on your website
  • Implement a proper cookie consent mechanism on your website
