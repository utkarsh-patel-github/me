How to Use

1

Enter event details

Type a name for your event and select the target date and time. Choose whether to display seconds and save the countdown for later use.

2

Start your countdown

Click the "Start Countdown" button to begin tracking the time remaining until your event. The countdown will update in real-time.

3

Save or share

Use the save option to store your countdown for future visits. Share your countdown with friends and family using the share button.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my countdown work if I close my browser?

Yes, if you check the "Save this countdown" option before starting. Your countdown will be stored locally on your device and will be available when you return to this page. No account required!

How accurate is the countdown timer?

Our countdown timer is highly accurate as it uses your device's internal clock. However, for absolute precision (within milliseconds), you may want to refresh the page closer to the target time.

Is there a limit to how many countdowns I can save?

There's no specific limit, but the number of countdowns you can save depends on your browser's local storage capacity. Typically, this allows for dozens of saved countdowns without issues.

What happens when the countdown reaches zero?

When a countdown ends, you'll see a celebratory message indicating your event has arrived. For saved countdowns, it will be automatically marked as expired but remains accessible from the "Saved Countdowns" tab.
