About Note Keeper

Note Keeper is a free online notepad that helps you create, edit, and organize your notes. Whether you're jotting down quick ideas, making to-do lists, or saving important information, Note Keeper makes it easy to keep track of everything.

Key Features:

  • Auto-Save: Never lose your work with automatic saving
  • Rich Text Formatting: Format your notes with bold, italic, lists, and more
  • Categories: Organize notes into categories like Personal, Work, Ideas, and To-Dos
  • Search: Quickly find notes with the built-in search feature
  • Local Storage: Your notes are saved in your browser's local storage
  • Export Options: Export your notes as text files
  • Dark Mode Support: Comfortable writing in any lighting conditions

Note Keeper is designed to be a simple yet powerful tool for all your note-taking needs, completely free and without requiring any account creation.

How to Use Note Keeper

Getting started with Note Keeper is simple:

Creating a New Note

  1. Click the "New Note" button in the sidebar
  2. Give your note a title (or keep "Untitled Note")
  3. Start typing in the content area
  4. Optionally select a category from the dropdown
  5. Your note is automatically saved as you type

Formatting Your Notes

Use the toolbar above the editor to format your text:

  • Text formatting: Bold, Italic, Underline, Strikethrough
  • Alignment: Left, Center, Right
  • Lists: Bulleted and Numbered lists
  • Insert: Links and Images

Managing Your Notes

  • Filter notes by category using the sidebar
  • Search for specific notes using the search bar
  • Delete unwanted notes with the Delete button
  • Export notes to save them outside the browser

Note: Your notes are stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data will remove your notes, so make sure to export important notes regularly.

Privacy & Data

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how Note Keeper handles your data:

Local Storage Only

All your notes are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your notes never leave your device
  • We don't have access to your notes
  • No account creation or login required
  • No server-side storage or processing

Important Considerations

  • Clearing your browser data/cache will delete your notes
  • Notes are device-specific and not synced across devices
  • For important notes, we recommend using the Export feature regularly

Data Limitations

Local storage has limited capacity (typically 5-10MB depending on your browser). If you store many notes or include images, you may eventually reach this limit. The app will notify you if you're approaching storage limits.

FAQ

Are my notes private?

Yes, your notes are completely private. They are stored only in your browser's local storage and never sent to any server. Only you can access them on your device.

Will I lose my notes if I clear my browser data?

Yes. Since notes are stored in local storage, clearing your browser data/cache will delete them. We recommend exporting important notes regularly using the Export feature.

Is there a limit to how many notes I can create?

Your browser's local storage has a limit (typically 5-10MB). The number of notes you can create depends on their size. Text-only notes are small, but including images will use more storage. The app will notify you if you're approaching the limit.

Can I access my notes on different devices?

No. Notes are stored in your browser's local storage, which is specific to the device and browser you're using. To access notes on another device, you would need to export them from one device and import them on another.
