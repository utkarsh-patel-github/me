About Terms of Service

Terms of Service (ToS) is a legal agreement between your service and its users. It's essential for:

  • Protecting your business from legal issues
  • Setting clear rules for using your service
  • Limiting your liability
  • Establishing user responsibilities

Important Notes:

  • This tool generates a basic terms of service template
  • Consider having a legal professional review the terms
  • Keep your terms updated as your service evolves
  • Make sure the terms are easily accessible on your website
