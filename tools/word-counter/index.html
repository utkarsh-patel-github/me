Frequently Asked Questions

How is the word count calculated?

Our tool counts words by identifying sequences of characters separated by spaces or punctuation. Hyphenated words (like "well-being") count as a single word. Numbers and special characters alone don't count as words.

What counts as a sentence?

Sentences are text segments ending with a period (.), question mark (?), exclamation point (!), or similar terminal punctuation. The tool intelligently distinguishes between periods used in abbreviations (like "Dr.") and those ending sentences.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average reading speed of 200-250 words per minute for adults reading in their native language. It's a useful approximation for how long it would take to read your text aloud or silently.

Is my text saved or stored?

No, your privacy is important to us. All text processing happens directly in your browser. We don't save, store, or share any content you enter, upload, or analyze with our tool.
